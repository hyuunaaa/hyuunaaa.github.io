*Student Classification: The KAIST Graduate School of Information Security offers different student classifications, including government scholarship students, CSP (Convergence Security Program) students, and CSPN (National Security Research Institute employment-linked) students. Each classification has different benefits like tuition support and obligations.

Application Process: KAIST's application consists of simple self-introduction forms, including nine short answers and one essay. Candidates may submit supplementary materials like recommendation letters and publications.

Interview: Candidates undergo a 20-minute interview focusing on technical knowledge and research experience.
Post-Admission: Research advisor contact is made after admission results are released
