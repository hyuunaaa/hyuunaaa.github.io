▶ **Osaka Castle**  
If your accommodation is near Namba Station, start your journey here. One of Japan's three great castles, the basic walking course takes 1-2 hours. Don't miss a photo at the main tower!

✅ Entrance Fee: 600 yen  
✅ Hours: 09:00-17:00  

▶ **Hep Five Ferris Wheel**  
A great spot for shopping nearby. The Ferris wheel offers 15-minute rides with Bluetooth speakers. Visit during sunset for a unique experience without long waits.

▶ **Umeda Sky Building**  
A must-see for Osaka’s night view with a 360-degree observation deck. Free with the Osaka Pass until 6 PM.
